Problem definition

  • Poaching in Africa is a serious problem: i.e. in 10 years time (2004-2014) the forest elephant population declined by

    78-81% -- more than 25,000 elephants

    (Poulsen et al., 2017)

  • If no policies will be introduced, the rhino population in Africa will become

    extinct in 2035

    (Barichievy et al., 2017)
Lack of data regarding

poachers and their locations

Goal for the project

  • Gain insight in potential poaching behaviour
  • Create a platform that uses RL to create new policy standards, train them and visualize their effects
  • Provide potential policies and their results after traning
  • Do research within RL and poaching behaviour

Allow further research to build onwards towards stopping poaching!

Reinforcement Learning -

but WHY?
  • Technology that simulates potential strategies for agents within the environment
  • Machine learning that is not dependent on data - but generates data!
  • Allows simulations within certain policies by using reward systems and states for the agents
  • Being able to simplify the problem showcases understanding of the problem!
